Welcome – The Urgency in 2026

Global warming is accelerating. 2024 was the warmest year, 2025 ranked 2nd or 3rd. The three-year average (2023–2025) exceeded 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels for the first time.

Quick Facts

  • Human activity (fossil fuels, deforestation) is the dominant cause
  • Rising temperatures → melting ice, stronger storms, biodiversity collapse
  • Impacts already visible: heat deaths, wildfires, floods, food insecurity
